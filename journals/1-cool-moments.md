## Cool moments
- **Pitch tint (iter 5)** — added pitchTint = (pitchClassNormalized-0.5)*0.04*quietGate to chromadepth() hue. The note nudges the WHOLE palette by ±0.02 (far under one depth band) so color breathes with melody while red=near/violet=far ordering holds.
