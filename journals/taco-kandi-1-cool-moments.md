## Why this base
- User wanted coat effects on the taco, then asked to add plasma as a base too.
- Plasma was the canonical "matter inside" the gravitational well — fits the kandi-bracelet "look into the taco" vibe.
- Coat contributed the silhouette-masking discipline (clean black outside, effects gated to inside).

## Key lessons carried (from plasma + the-coat journals)
- **Slow modulations breathe as one organism** — share base frequency (0.4Hz here for SHAPE_DRIFT).
- **Use #define swap pattern** — every audio param has a commented constant alternate.
- **Statistical variations matter** — *ZScore for events, *Normalized for smooth modulation, *Mean/*Median for slow drift.
- **Silhouette discipline** — outside the taco mask is pure black; chrome rim only fires on the ink strokes; god rays gated to silhouette.
- **VOID_DARKNESS bug avoided** — using monotonic envelope for lensing, not inverting smoothstep thresholds.
- **Knob auto-wire on >0.4 Δ in one tick** — gestures get wired same tick.
