## Design hypotheses for v(next)
- **Dr-fresch corner needs dedicated bass-weighted treatment.** This shader's HEART PULSE retune is the prototype: `sin(time * sub-1Hz) * 0.15` amplitude breath × bass-Z-spike thump = calm monster. Reusable pattern for other bass-driven blocks.
- **User-knob-wiggle = explicit user gesture.** The auto-wire principle (C.2 in the skill) was promoted in iter 14 when knob_16 was mapped to THEME_SHIFT on the user's request. v(next) should bake "the wiggling knob is an intent signal" deeper into the design — a knob the user grabs is a knob the user expects to do something meaningful.
- **Slow modulations vs fast modulations** read as opposite emotional states. Sub-1Hz = brooding, weighty, monster. 5Hz+ = jittery, anxious, sparkly. Match modulation frequency to emotional intent.
