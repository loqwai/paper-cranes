- **Iter 19b of plasma-event-horizon/3 (2026-05-02) — STROBING FIX (user: "It's flashing black like every other frame"):** Iter 19's `RADIATION_WAVES(r) = sin(r * 9.0 - iTime * RADIATION_LOUDNESS * RATE * TAU)` had a critical bug: **the phase argument depended on audio**. Fix: take `RADIATION_LOUDNESS` OUT of the phase argument. This is the GLSL equivalent of the "phase-locked vs amplitude-modulated" distinction in DSP.

## Design hypotheses for v(next)
- Sub-Hz coprime oscillators inside the SDF/map function are the right pattern for "evolving fractal" — they morph the structure aperiodically without snap-back.
- Knob-controlled rate multiplier on autonomous oscillators (FRAC_EVO_RATE) gives users a "freeze ↔ accelerate" lever that complements the audio-reactive layer cleanly.
- For radial composition fades, prefer monotonic `1.0 - smoothstep(inner, outer, r)` over inverted-direction `smoothstep(outer, inner, r)` — the former is easier to verify visually and resistant to knob-wiring bugs.
