## What 2.frag does

- Red = closest (focal point), blue/violet = farthest (background)
- Drop detection via `energySlope * energyRSquared` → spotlight effect
- Lace-pattern from orbit traps as the only "lit" content; background stays deep velvety purple
- Rim lighting on body-silhouette edges, warmth modulated by `spectralRoughnessNormalized`
- Beat flash + bass pulse + frame feedback (FEEDBACK_MIX)
