# handstand-portal/2 — Subdocta-tuned fork

## Origin
Forked from `handstand-portal/1` on 2026-06-12 during a live `/vibej` run (iter 14), tuned for a **Subdocta show** (heavy dubstep/riddim). Audio came through a virtual mic. Fully audio-reactive — **no knobs set** (all `knob_*` empty); every parameter is driven by wavelet/FFT features.

## Why this fork
`1.frag` accumulated 14 live VJ iterations turning the base chromadepth handstand into a full-spectrum, Subdocta-ready, depth-band-correct reactive figure — plus the figure-blur fix. `2.frag` snapshots that good state so further experiments don't risk the working version.

## What it does (all chromadepth-safe — red=near, green=mid, blue/violet=far)
**Bass / drops (the headline):**
- `FIGURE_BOUNCE` — kick lifts the silhouette (a hop)
- `FIGURE_WOBBLE` — `wubPulse`×`wubDepth` sways the body side-to-side (dubstep wobble signature)
- `FIGURE_SHAKE` — convulsive rotation about center on kicks/wub/roughness
- `bassSurge` — bass scrolls the interior plasma upward
- `BASS BLOOM` — kick-gated red halo, dark passages only
- `DROP PUNCH` — energy-surge + kick zooms the whole frame IN (figure lunges at viewer)

**Mids:**
- `MID WARMTH` — mids-over-centroid swells a warm interior glow

**Treble:**
- `TREBLE SHIMMER` — limbs (green extremities) twinkle on airy passages
- `SCOOP HEAT` — screechy scooped-mids leads heat the rim red→red-orange
- star sparkle — far-field stars twinkle faster/brighter with treble

**Texture / palette:**
- `ENTROPY FRACTURE` — high entropy sharpens nebula into crystalline filaments
- `CALM RECEDE` — quiet tonal passages push field toward violet + dim
- `pitchTint` — note nudges the whole palette ±0.02 (depth order preserved)

**Readability fix:**
- Figure coverage now tracks the REAL silhouette with a tight `fwidth`-based AA edge (the blurry interior `fill` no longer dilutes coverage). Always reads crisply as "girl doing a one-handed handstand."
