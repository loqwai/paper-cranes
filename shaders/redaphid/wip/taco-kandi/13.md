- BASS_PEAK now stacks 5 weighted continuous signals (TREND + BASSSM + drop_glow + beat_kick + INSTANT) instead of MAX-of-six instants. Smoother envelope.
- ZOOM_INTENSITY ceiling tightened (1.7 → 1.5) so peak doesn't over-contract on extreme stack-up.
