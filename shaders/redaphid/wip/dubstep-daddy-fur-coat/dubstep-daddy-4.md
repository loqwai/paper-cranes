# lonely — Powerful But Alone

## Origin

Forked from `dubstep-daddy-3.frag` on 2026-04-14 during "Love Is Gone (Acoustic)" by SLANDER, Dylan Matthew.

The user wanted a variant that emphasizes being alone — powerful but sad. The figure is small in the frame, the colors are cool, the motion is slow. The coat is the only warmth.

## Vibe

- Zoomed way out — the figure is small and alone in darkness
- Cool blue-violet palette instead of hot pink
- Slow, breathing motion — not bouncing, swaying gently
- Eyes barely glow — embers, not fire
- God rays dim — no spectacle, just quiet presence
- The fractal fur coat is still the star — the one beautiful thing

## What Changed from dubstep-daddy-3

- Zoom floor pulled back (0.15 vs 0.3) — figure is smaller
- Energy breathing reduced (0.15 vs 0.4) — less reactive zoom
- Pump halved (0.3 vs 0.6) — barely bouncing
- Beat phase slowed (1.4 vs 2.2) — everything moves slower
- Snap reduced to 30% — almost still
- Hip sway halved and slowed — gentle rocking
- Groove halved — subtle body movement
- Drop zoom gentle (0.3 vs 0.9) — slow inhale not punch
- God rays dimmed — max 1.0x not 2.5x
- Eye wash dimmed — max 0.2 not 0.5
- Base hue shifted to blue-violet (0.65 vs 0.78)
- Drop hue shifted to pale lavender (0.72 vs 0.08) — cold not hot
